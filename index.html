Lee-Bella's Promise Haven

Restoring dignity. Rebuilding lives. Renewing hope.

Our Mission

Lee-Bella's Promise Haven supports individuals experiencing homelessness by removing barriers to employment and stability through outreach, transportation assistance, and connections to essential services.

What We Do

  • Transportation assistance for job interviews and services
  • Community outreach and stabilization support
  • Connections to housing and employment resources

Our Impact Goal

Our goal is to assist at least 50 individuals in our first year by helping them access employment, essential services, and pathways to stable housing.

Get Involved

Volunteer, partner with us, or support our mission to help individuals rebuild their lives with dignity and opportunity.
