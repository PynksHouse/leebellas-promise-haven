Our Mission

Lee-Bella’s Promise Haven is a community initiative dedicated to helping individuals experiencing homelessness regain stability and independence.

Our goal is to remove barriers that prevent people from accessing employment, healthcare, and essential services.

Through outreach efforts, transportation assistance, and connections to community resources, we aim to help individuals rebuild their lives and move toward long-term stability.

How You Can Help

Your support helps provide transportation assistance, outreach supplies, and community support for individuals experiencing homelessness.
