About

Practical support, compassionate care, measurable outcomes.

Mission

The mission of Lee-Bella’s Promise Haven is to support individuals experiencing homelessness by removing barriers to employment and stability through outreach, transportation assistance, and resource connections that empower people to regain independence and dignity.

Founder

Founded by Tanika Elease Brown, Lee-Bella’s Promise Haven was created to help individuals access real opportunities—jobs, services, and housing pathways—by eliminating practical barriers that keep people stuck in survival mode.

Programs

What we provide through outreach, support, and partnerships.

Outreach & Stabilization

Meet people where they are

We connect directly in the community, identify needs, and guide individuals to resources that support stability.

Transportation Assistance

Access to jobs & appointments

We provide public transit support so individuals can attend interviews, maintain employment, and access essential services.

Resource Navigation

Connections that matter

We connect individuals to workforce programs, benefits support, healthcare resources, and housing pathways.

Impact

How we measure progress and strengthen the community.

2026 Goals

Serve 50+ individuals through outreach, transportation assistance, and resource connections.

We track: people served, transportation support distributed, job access supported, and successful referrals to services.

Community Benefit

When people can access work and essential services, stability improves. This supports the local economy, reduces strain on emergency systems, and strengthens community well-being.
