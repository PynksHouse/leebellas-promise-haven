About

Built on compassion, practicality, and measurable outcomes.

Mission

The mission of Lee-Bella’s Promise Haven is to support individuals experiencing homelessness by removing barriers to employment and stability through outreach, transportation assistance, and resource connections that empower people to regain independence and dignity.

Founder

Founded by Tanika Elease Brown, Lee-Bella’s Promise Haven was created to make it easier for people to access real opportunities— not just temporary relief—through consistent support, guidance, and community partnerships.

Programs

Direct support that removes barriers and connects people to opportunity.

Outreach & Stabilization

Meet people where they are

We connect with individuals in the community, identify immediate needs, and provide guidance toward services that support stability.

Transportation Assistance

Access to jobs & appointments

We provide public transit support so individuals can attend job interviews, maintain employment, and access essential services.

Resource Navigation

Connections that matter

We connect individuals to workforce programs, benefits enrollment support, healthcare resources, and housing pathways.

Impact

What we track, measure, and improve.

2026 Pilot Outcomes

Our goal is to directly support 50+ individuals in 2026 through transportation assistance, outreach, and resource connections.

  • People served through outreach
  • Transportation assistance distributed
  • Job interview / employment access supported
  • Referrals to services completed
Why this helps the community

When people can access work and essential services, stability improves. This supports the local economy, reduces strain on emergency systems, and strengthens overall community well-being.

Future expansion includes deeper partnerships and a supportive housing pathway as funding grows.
